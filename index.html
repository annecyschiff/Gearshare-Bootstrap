Welcome to Gearshare!

Gearshare aims to increase access to outdoor gear in communities and offers a platform to maximize the productivity of outdoor products for their owners. Use Gearshare to rent out yoru gear or rent others gear, make a gear swap with another Gearshare member, or connect with other adventurers near you and benefit from the outdoors knowledge and skills in your community.
